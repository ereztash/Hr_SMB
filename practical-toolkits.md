## TEMPLATE 1: Positioning Statement Builder (Iterative)

### Version 1 — Generic (This is where most start)
```
I help [broad audience] with [broad problem]

Example: "I help tech leaders with organizational challenges"
Problem: Could be 500+ consultants
```

### Version 2 — Narrower
```
I help [specific audience] with [specific problem]

Example: "I help CTOs at Series A-C companies reduce time-to-hire and improve retention"
Better: But still not unique
```

### Version 3 — Differentiated (Blue Ocean)
```
I help [specific audience] navigate [specific situation], by [unique method], 
resulting in [measurable outcome], in [timeframe], unlike [Red Ocean competitors who do X]

Example: "I help CTOs at Series A-C companies reduce time-to-hire from 4 months to 8 weeks 
through structure-first org design (design before hiring), unlike recruiters who optimize hiring 
processes after org is locked in"

Better: Specific, unique, measurable, competitive clarity
```

### Version 4 — Authentic (Grounded in Real Experience)
```
I do this because [genuine reason from lived experience]

Example: "I do this because I spent 10 years as a recruiter watching great talent leave companies 
not due to hiring failure, but due to broken org structure. Now I fix the structure first, 
making hiring 3x easier and retention automatic."

Best: Credible, memorable, authentic
```

### Version 5 — Social Proof Integrated
```
I help [specific audience] ... 
Proof: [specific results from real clients]
Clients: [name/type of 1-2 best clients]

Example: "I help CTOs at Series A-C companies reduce time-to-hire by 40% through 
structure-first org design. Recent clients: Acme Fintech (reduced hiring time 50%), 
XYZ B2B SaaS (improved 3-year retention from 35% to 18%)"

Best: Credibility + specificity combined
```

## TEMPLATE 3: 30-Day Signal Building Sprint

### Goal: Build ONE costly signal in 30 days

**Signal Type (choose one):**
- [ ] Case study (get 1 client to agree to documentation)
- [ ] Published article (5,000 words on your specialty)
- [ ] Speaking proposal (apply to 5 conferences)
- [ ] Shipped MVP (build 1 small but real project)

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Choose specific signal
- [ ] Outline scope (if article: outline, thesis, data needed)
- [ ] Gather resources (if case study: interview client, get metrics)
- [ ] Set weekly milestones

### Week 2-3: Core Work
- [ ] Execute: Write, build, or develop
- [ ] Daily progress tracking (30 min/day minimum)
- [ ] Get feedback from 1 peer (mid-project checkpoint)

### Week 4: Refinement & Launch
- [ ] Polish (edit, test, review)
- [ ] Publish/submit/launch
- [ ] Promote (share with network, tag relevant people)
- [ ] Track metrics (views, engagement, inbound inquiries)

## TEMPLATE 5: LinkedIn Profile Optimization Checklist

### Headline (120 characters)
- [ ] Includes specific audience
- [ ] Includes specific outcome
- [ ] Includes unique method (not just "consultant")
- [ ] No jargon or buzzwords

**Optimized example:**
> ✅ "I help CTOs reduce time-to-hire by 40% through org design | Scaling distributed teams"

**vs. Generic example:**
> ❌ "CTO | Executive Coach | Thought Leader | Open to networking"

### Profile Summary (2,000 characters)
- [ ] Opens with WHO you help, WHAT problem you solve
- [ ] Includes 1-2 measurable outcomes
- [ ] Includes origin story / why you do this
- [ ] Links to portfolio/case studies/best work
- [ ] CTA at bottom (book call, download resource, etc.)

### Experience Section
- [ ] Each role highlights: Scope, outcomes, unique contribution
- [ ] Not just titles/responsibilities
- [ ] Includes metrics where possible

**Optimized example:**
> Built org design methodology adopted by 12 Series A-C companies, reducing time-to-hire by 40% and improving 3-year retention to 85%+

**vs. Generic example:**
> "Worked as consultant on organizational projects"

### Recommendations & Endorsements
- [ ] 3+ specific recommendations highlighting your specialty
- [ ] Endorsements focused on your differentiation (not generic "leadership")

## TEMPLATE 7: Signal-Building Checklist (By Type)

### If Building: Case Study
- [ ] Client identified and permission granted
- [ ] Before state: What was the situation/problem?
- [ ] Approach: What specifically did you do?
- [ ] After state: What changed? (with metrics)
- [ ] Timeline: How long did transformation take?
- [ ] Client testimonial: Specific quote (not generic)
- [ ] Learning: What can others learn from this?
- [ ] Published: On website, LinkedIn, downloadable
- [ ] Promoted: Emailed network, tagged relevant people

**Effort:** 20-30 hours  
**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks  
**ROI:** High (very credible signal)

### If Building: Published Article
- [ ] Topic: Specific, not generic
- [ ] Thesis: Clear argument, not just commentary
- [ ] Data: Original research, survey, or analysis (not just opinion)
- [ ] Outline: 3-5 main points with supporting evidence
- [ ] Length: 3,000-5,000 words (substantial)
- [ ] Examples: Real case examples (anonymized if needed)
- [ ] Published: Target Towards Data Science, Medium, or own blog
- [ ] Promoted: Shared with network, tagged influencers in field

**Effort:** 40-60 hours  
**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks  
**ROI:** Medium-High (good for building authority)

### If Building: Speaking Submission
- [ ] Target conferences: 5-10 venues identified
- [ ] Talk title: Specific, compelling (not generic)
- [ ] Abstract: 100-200 word summary with learning objective
- [ ] Outline: 30-min talk structure with main points
- [ ] Speaker bio: 2-3 sentences with credibility
- [ ] Submitted: To all venues
- [ ] Follow-up: Track acceptance, prepare if accepted

**Effort:** 10-20 hours  
**Timeline:** Ongoing (apply every quarter)  
**ROI:** High (credibility + network + inbound)
